Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
Solar Dashboard does not collect, store on any server, or share any personal data with third parties. Ever.

1. What data is used

Solar Dashboard uses the following data solely to provide its features:

  • GPS location — used on-device to calculate the sun's position and angle relative to your solar panels, and to request local weather data from Open-Meteo. Your coordinates are stored locally on your device (Android SharedPreferences) so the app remembers your location between sessions. They are never stored on any external server.
  • Weather data — your GPS coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo (a free, open-source weather API) to retrieve local solar irradiance, temperature and cloud cover. Open-Meteo does not require an account and does not associate coordinates with any personal identity.
  • Energy readings — consumption values, battery state of charge, and system configuration you enter are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

2. What data is NOT collected

  • No account or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No crash reporting sent to external servers
  • No data sold or shared with third parties

3. Permissions explained

  • Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) — required to calculate your solar panel's sun angle and fetch local weather. Without this the app cannot estimate production.
  • Internet (INTERNET) — required to fetch weather data from Open-Meteo and load the app interface.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — optional. Used only to send local alerts about solar production windows and appliance scheduling. No notification data is sent to external servers.

4. Third-party services

The only external service contacted by Solar Dashboard is Open-Meteo, an open-source weather API. Only your GPS coordinates are sent, with no personal identifiers. Please review Open-Meteo's terms for their data handling practices.

5. Data retention

All data (location cache, energy logs, configuration) is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences and localStorage. It is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app. You can clear it at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Solar Dashboard → Storage → Clear Data.

6. Children's privacy

Solar Dashboard does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, no social features, and no data collection of any kind.

7. Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact the developer at the email address listed on the Google Play Store page for Solar Dashboard.
